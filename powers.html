Professor Rambo's Capabilities

"Super Powers" by darkday. is licensed under CC BY 2.0

There are various things Profressor Rambo is capable of. This obviously separates him from people on Earth, but also on Vortex 1214b. Potentially, he can be labled a mutant. The powers he posesses are teleportation, levitation, and telekinesis. He can bend and lift objects as well as reading peoples' minds. When combining his powers and him being wickedly smart, he can cause distruction to virtually anthing. This allows him to adapt to various situation because he can operate like a fast CPU.
